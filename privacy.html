Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Nollistudio ("we," "us," "our") collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Nollistudio tool suite ("the Service") — a locally-hosted set of applications for scheduling, coordinating, and collaborating on social-media content across Instagram, Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, and Twitter / X.

Contact: team@nollistudio.com

Who we are

Nollistudio builds a family of creative-production tools: Nolli Scheduler (this Meta app), the Visual Board Designer, Moodboard, Shorts Creator, and the Review Portal. The tools run on your own computer or within a Dropbox folder you control; they are not a cloud service we operate on your behalf.

What data the Service accesses

When you connect your social-media accounts, the Service accesses only the data strictly required to schedule and publish content on your behalf. Per platform, this may include:

  • Your account identifier (user ID, Page ID, Instagram Business Account ID) and display name
  • OAuth access tokens issued to the Service by the platform
  • Content you create, upload, or draft within the Service — videos, images, captions, titles, scheduled dates
  • Public engagement metrics returned by the platform's API for posts you publish (likes, comments, views, plays) — used to drive A/B test winner selection when you opt into Trial Reels
  • For Instagram Trial Reels specifically: the ID of a trial reel you have created and its public permalink, used to graduate the winning variant to the main feed once an A/B test concludes

The Service does not access your contacts, direct messages, private data belonging to other users, followers' personal information, or anything unrelated to publishing content you have created.

Where your data lives

All data collected by the Service is stored locally on your device or in a shared folder you control — typically your own Dropbox account. Nollistudio does not operate a central server that stores, proxies, or sees your data, your tokens, or your content.

A limited amount of data (post metadata + engagement-based winner selection results) is stored on a Cloudflare-hosted review portal we operate for optional collaboration features, only when you explicitly share a project with a reviewer. You can purge this data at any time via the Delete button in the review portal dashboard, or by emailing team@nollistudio.com.

How we use your data

Data accessed by the Service is used solely to:

  1. Authenticate you with the social platforms you have connected
  2. Schedule, publish, and optionally A/B-test the content you create
  3. Display the analytics you have explicitly requested
  4. Coordinate between multiple workstations you have configured (via the shared folder you own)

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for advertising or analytics purposes. We do not use your data to train machine-learning models.

Third parties

When you schedule or publish content, the Service transmits the content and its metadata to the social platforms you have selected (Meta / Instagram / Facebook, YouTube, TikTok, LinkedIn, Twitter / X). Those platforms are independent data controllers and their own privacy policies govern what they do with the content once published.

The Service uses the following external services, each limited to the request-scoped purpose stated:

Data retention

Local data is retained for as long as you use the Service. There is no automatic expiry. You can delete it at any time by clearing the application's working directory, uninstalling the Service, and revoking its access from each platform.

Review-portal data is retained until you delete it or email us to delete it. Access tokens stored in the local `tokens.json` file rotate according to each platform's token-expiry policy.

Your rights

You can at any time:

  1. Revoke the Service's access to any connected platform via that platform's authorized-apps settings. For Instagram: Settings → Apps and websites → Active → Remove "Nolli Scheduler". This invalidates any tokens we hold.
  2. Delete all local data by uninstalling the Service and clearing its working directory on disk.
  3. Request deletion of review-portal data by emailing team@nollistudio.com from the address associated with your account, or by using the Delete button in the review portal dashboard.
  4. Ask us questions about what data we hold by emailing team@nollistudio.com. We will respond within 30 days.

For step-by-step deletion instructions, see our Data Deletion Instructions.

Children's privacy

The Service is not intended for use by anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, email team@nollistudio.com and we will delete it.

Changes to this policy

If we make material changes to this policy, we will update the Last updated date at the top of the page and — where practical — notify affected users. Continued use of the Service after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Governing law

This policy is governed by the laws of the jurisdiction in which Nollistudio is registered. Disputes that cannot be resolved informally are subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the competent courts in that jurisdiction.

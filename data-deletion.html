Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: 24 April 2026

This page explains how to delete your data from the Nollistudio tool suite and revoke the Service's access to your connected accounts.

Because the Service stores its data locally on your own device (and, where you opt in, in a Dropbox folder you own), most of the deletion work happens on your side — we do not run a central server that holds your data. The one exception is the optional collaboration review portal, covered in the last section.

1. Revoke the Service's access on each platform

This step invalidates any access tokens Nollistudio holds for your accounts. Do it on every platform you have connected.

Instagram

  1. Open Instagram and log in to the account that authorized the Service
  2. Tap your profile → menu → Settings and privacy
  3. Scroll to Apps and websites
  4. Under the Active tab, find Nolli Scheduler-IG (or Nolli Scheduler)
  5. Tap Remove

Direct URL (sign in first): instagram.com/accounts/manage_access

Facebook

  1. Open Facebook and log in to the account that authorized the Service
  2. Go to Settings & privacy → Settings → Apps and websites
  3. Under Active, find Nolli Scheduler
  4. Click Remove

Direct URL: facebook.com/settings?tab=business_tools

YouTube / Google

  1. Go to myaccount.google.com/permissions
  2. Find Nolli Scheduler in the list
  3. Click Remove Access

TikTok

  1. Open TikTok → profile → menu → Settings and privacy → Security & login → Manage app permissions
  2. Find Nolli Scheduler
  3. Tap Remove

LinkedIn

  1. Go to Settings & Privacy → Data privacy → Other applications → Permitted services
  2. Find Nolli Scheduler
  3. Click Remove

Twitter / X

  1. Go to x.com/settings/connected_apps
  2. Find Nolli Scheduler
  3. Click Revoke access

2. Delete local data on your device

Once you have revoked the Service's platform access, delete the Service's working files from your computer:

  1. Quit the Nolli Scheduler and any other Nollistudio tool still running
  2. Open the folder where the tools store their data (default locations):
    • Application file: wherever you installed the single-file NS_SCHEDULER.html / NS_BOARD_DESIGNER_V3.html
    • Server-side state: ~/Dropbox/NOLLI_SCHEDULER/server/ (contains tokens.json, promote-queue.json, scheduler-role.json, trial-sync.json, etc.)
    • Browser storage: the Service uses IndexedDB (nolli-scheduler database) for local state. Clear it via your browser's developer tools → Application → IndexedDB → right-click the database → Delete
  3. Delete the tool files and the above state files
  4. Empty your system trash

3. Delete data in the Review Portal (optional)

If you use the collaboration Review Portal, project data is stored in Nollistudio's Cloudflare-hosted infrastructure only for projects you have explicitly shared. To delete:

  • Log in to the Review Portal and click Delete project for each project you want removed, or
  • Email team@nollistudio.com from the address associated with your account, requesting deletion. We will confirm and complete deletion within 30 days.

4. Request a data access report

You can also ask us to confirm, in writing, what data (if any) we hold about you in the Review Portal. Email team@nollistudio.com and include the account or email address you used to connect the Service. We will respond within 30 days.

Still have questions?

Email team@nollistudio.com. We aim to respond to deletion and access requests within one business day and will confirm completion within 30 days of the request.
